Welcome to my blog.

I've been meaning to start writing for a while. Not to build an audience or grow a brand — just to think more clearly. Writing forces precision. You can't half-understand something and write about it convincingly.

## What I'll write about

Mostly software engineering: systems design, programming languages, tools I use, things I've built, and lessons learned the hard way. Occasionally something outside of tech if it's interesting enough.

## Why Hugo

Static sites are fast, cheap to host, and require no database. Hugo builds in milliseconds and deploys to GitHub Pages for free. It gets out of the way so I can focus on writing.

## What's next

I'll write when I have something worth saying. No schedule, no commitment to consistency — just posts when they're ready.
